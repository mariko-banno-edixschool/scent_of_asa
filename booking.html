WORKSHOP FAQ

DO I NEED PRIOR EXPERIENCE?

None needed. Our workshop is designed for beginners.

CAN I JOIN ALONE?

Yes, solo travelers are always welcome at our studio.

WHAT DO I TAKE HOME?

One 50ml bottle of your custom-blended fragrance.

IS THE WORKSHOP IN ENGLISH?

Yes, we provide full instruction in fluent English.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE?

The full curated experience lasts 90 minutes.
